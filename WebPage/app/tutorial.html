Checking out

  1. While viewing a listing, click the button to "Add to Cart" the item to your cart
  2. Click on "purchase" to complete your transactions
  3. Click purchase to check out and begin payment transactions through link

Creating a Post

NOTE: these same steps can be followed for creating a listing

  1. Click on the "+" icon
  2. Click on the posts button
  3. Upload image(s), and fill out the Name and Caption with your desired values
  4. Now click "post" to upload it to your followers!

Linking listing to a post

  1. Click on the "+" icon
  2. Click on the Listing button
  3. Upload images fill out fields (price, Gender, Size, Description, Brand, Condition, Colors, Tags)
  4. Click on link posts section and select the desired post

Becoming a Seller

  1. Click the 'plus' icon on the bottom navigation bar
  2. Click the 'register now' button

Using our Generative AI feature for listings

  1. Follow the same steps for Creating a post, upload photos and fill out the form fields press the 'Generate AI tags' button.
  2. Once these are generated, you can manually add and remove tags.
