Emma Luk

Emma Luk is a full-stack developer pursuing a B.S. in Software Development with a minor in Entrepreneurship at the University of Utah. She is the founder of SOUR, a social eCommerce app that enhances second-hand fashion through video-based listings and AI-generated descriptions, built with Python, React Native, and AWS.

She has led development as Code Lead for SEEDS Dandilyonn, a food community app, and competed in the Information Systems Game Day Analytics Challenge, analyzing Super Bowl Twitter data for marketing insights. Her technical skills include Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, FastAPI, Firebase, and AWS.

Emma has worked as a Teaching Assistant at the Kahlert School of Computing and interned at Mapps Lab. She is a Kahlert Scholar, Get Seeded Grant recipient, and active in the Society of Women Engineers. Passionate about innovation, she builds user-centric applications to solve real-world problems.
